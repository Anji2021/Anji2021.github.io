Overview:

Led the end-to-end development of an enterprise-wide Onboarding Analytics Platform to streamline and optimize the hiring and onboarding process across 10+ clinical divisions in a 180-hospital system. The platform addressed slow, manual workflows that delayed staffing for critical hospital roles, directly impacting operational efficiency and patient care capacity.

Key Challenges:

  • Over 3,000 monthly hires across diverse clinical and non-clinical roles.
  • Manual, repetitive processes causing onboarding delays of up to 4+ weeks per hire.
  • Lack of visibility into bottlenecks and SLA compliance across divisions.
  • Need to maintain strict compliance with HR policies and credentialing standards.

Approach:

  • Conducted process audits of 3,000+ monthly hires to identify bottlenecks and repetitive tasks.
  • Partnered with engineering, UX, and data science teams to design automated workflows and dashboards.
  • Defined KPIs and implemented real-time SLA monitoring dashboards for operational teams.
  • Aligned cross-functional stakeholders, including division leaders, HR coordinators, and executives, to ensure adoption and operational readiness.
  • Leveraged analytics to prioritize automation opportunities and provide actionable insights to leadership.

Impact & Results:

  • Reduced coordinator processing time from 55 minutes to 15 minutes per hire (73% efficiency gain).
  • Accelerated overall onboarding timelines by 38%, enabling faster staffing for 100+ critical hospital roles.
  • Standardized workflows and improved transparency, predictability, and data-driven decision-making across the enterprise.
  • Strengthened workforce deployment velocity, improved operational readiness, and enhanced compliance tracking across divisions.

Key Learnings:

  • Taking end-to-end ownership and combining automation with data analytics can drastically improve operational efficiency.
  • Effective stakeholder alignment and real-time insights are critical for large-scale enterprise adoption.
  • Analytics-driven process redesign can significantly impact both business outcomes and employee experiences.
